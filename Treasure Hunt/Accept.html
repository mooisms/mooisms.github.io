The voice guides you to the lake near your camp. It tells you to look into the water, promising that there you will find Eve—and your treasure. As you peer into the lake, you see a woman and a child. The image shifts, becoming the elderly man from the long path. Then, briefly, you see home—before it all fades back into your own reflection.

Confused and slightly unsettled, you ask the voice to explain. It responds with the same quiet certainty as before

“Eve can be found anywhere—in a conversation, in a view, or in a decision. It does not need to shimmer or be gold to be considered treasure. That is why the poorest of men can be the richest. The rarest treasure lies within you. Its value is yours to decide."
